About Manipal University Dubai

Manipal University Dubai, a branch campus of the prestigious Manipal Academy of Higher Education (India), has been a leader in higher education in the UAE since 2000. Located in Dubai International Academic City, our university offers a global education experience with Indian excellence at its core.

Our 750,000 square foot campus features world-class infrastructure, including cutting-edge laboratories, digital libraries, media studios, and modern sports complexes. With over 2,000 students from more than 40 nationalities, we are proud of our culturally diverse and inclusive learning environment.

We offer a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in disciplines such as Business, Engineering, Information Technology, Media & Communication, Interior Design, Life Sciences, and more.
